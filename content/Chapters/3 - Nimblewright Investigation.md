### Inciting Incident
1. [[Dalakhar]] was attempting to meet with Renaer Neverember at Trollskull Manor. He was being tracked by the Gralhund, Zhentarim, Cassalanters, and possibly others.
2. A small team of Zhentarim agents led by [[Urstul Floxin]] attempted to waylay Dalakhar as he came down Trollskull Alley.
3. The [[Gralhund Nimblewright]], observing the scene from a nearby rooftop, used a necklace of fireballs to launch a fireball which kills Dalakhar and most of the Zhentarim agents, with the exception of Urstul Floxin (who barely survives, but is incapacitated).
4. The Gralhund Nimblewright jumped off the roof, dashed forward, rifled through Dalakhar’s pockets, and took the Stone of Golorr. It then ran off, returning to Gralhund Villa
5. Someone the party knows or cares about dies in the explosion.

### The Crime Scene
> Several bodies lay strewn about. Some people were clearly killed in the explosion, however several seem unharmed but unconscious.

**Key Revelation:** A Nimblewright was responsible for the explosion.

#### Zhentarim Corpses
- Some of the corpses have tattoos of black flying snakes, a mark of the Zhentarim.
- Casting _speak with dead_ on him will reveal:
	- that they want something called the [[Stone of Golorr]] that Dalakhar was carrying,
	- that they work for Urstul Floxin, and
	- that they came from Yellowspire

#### [[Dalakhar]]
- Dalakhar is dead.
- Casting _speak with dead_ on him will reveal that he stole something called the [[Stone of Golorr]] from [[Xanathar's Guild]].

#### [[Urstul Floxin]]
- Alive but unconscious. Once he regains consciousness, he tries to leave as quickly as possible.
- When questioned, he will give his real name, since he is still disoriented.
- Claims to be in the area to go to... (glances around, points to a store) The Book Wyrm's Treasure
- **Wisdom (Insight) - DC 13** - He's lying. If pressed, he'll say:
  > Look, I must have been hallucinating. But just after the explosion, I could have sworn I saw a mechanical angel of death moving among the bodies. I thought he was going to come for me next, but then it turned and ran away.
  
  > [!info]
  > Urstul doesn't actually believe it was an "angel of death," but he wants to present himself as a confused rube who just happened to be passing by.

> [!info]
> Urstul does have a black flying snake tattoo, but it is on his left breast and not visible normally.

#### [[Renaer Neverember]]
Ten to fifteen minutes after the explosion, Renaer Neverember shows up. He tells the players:
- Renaer Neverember arranged to meet with Dalakhar in Trollskull Alley
- Dalakhar was an agent working for Lord Neverember
- Lord Neverember assigned Dalakhar to keep an eye on Renear. Renaer would see him skulking around everywhere.
- A few weeks ago, Renaer noticed that Dalakhar abruptly disappear, and Renaer doesn't know where he's been. This happened before Renaer was kidnapped by the Zhentarim.
- Renaer doesn't know why Dalakhar wanted to meet. His message simply said that he was carrying something valuable for Lord Neverember, was unable to deliver it, and was hoping that Renaer could help.

### Searching for Nimblewrights

Once the PCs have the description of the mechanical man responsible for the attack, the next step is to figure out exactly what it was and where it came from.

See [[List of Nimblewright Owners]].

#### Research
**Intelligence (Arcana) - DC 13** - it was a
nimblewright, most likely built by the technomancers of Luskan and based on ancient Calishite designs of the Shoon Imperium. They had not previously been seen in Waterdeep and the Luskan technomancers have been reticent about sharing their secrets.

**Intelligence (Arcana) - DC 17** - Bowgentra Summertaen, Lady Master of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors, is known to have recently come into possession of one.

#### Canvassing
**Charisma (Investigation) - DC 13** - Two owners of Nimblewrights are revealed. For every two points of margin of success, they discover an additional owner.

#### Other Approaches
Reveal 1-2 nimblewright owners if the players do something clever.

### Questioning the Owners

**Revelation:** They're being purchased from Captain Zord of the Sea Maiden's Faire.

### Finding the owner
**Revelation:** The Gralhunds own the Nimblewright that was responsible for the explosion.

Before the players get to Gralhund Villa, they receive a letter from the Cassalanters, asking for the party's help saving their children.
