This site provides a heavily altered version of the Waterdeep: Dragon Heist campaign for Dungeons and Dragons 5e. It pulls alterations mainly from Justin Alexander's [Alexandrian Remix](https://thealexandrian.net/wordpress/41217/roleplaying-games/dragon-heist-remix-part-1-the-villains) of the campaign, but there are many more supplements and alterations online that I'd like to make available here.
